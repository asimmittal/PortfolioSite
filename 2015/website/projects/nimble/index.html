Background

Nimble Schedule approached me to work on the UX of their web and mobile applications. Their existing application was extremely nuanced with various settings and configurations. They wanted me to simplify their UX and add make their users more efficient. I worked on revamping the UX for three applications:

  • Mobile app for managers and employees
  • Web application (mainstay of their business)
  • Tablet based timeclock application

Please note that the images shown below constitute only a small portion of the work that was done. Higher fidelity samples might be provided on request.

Mobile

Here are a few screens I worked on from the mobile app. The challenging part of this app was to boil down all the features of their web app to a simpler, more frequently used set. This app allowed managers and employees to manage their shifts, trade shifts, message each other, pick up open shifts and allow them to check in or out of a specific location.

I used a timeline based feed to unify all activity around how people in a company were communicating and managing shifts. This idea took a large part of the UI, which was fragmented and brought it all together in a simple format that users preferred and understood.
