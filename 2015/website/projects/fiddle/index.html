Goal

As part of my graduate curriculum at Carnegie Mellon University, I put together a video sketch around the concept of interactive surfaces in public spaces. My team chose the coffee shop as our venue of choice as they are places that bring together a lot of people with varying needs.

This project showcases my ability to build a narrative around an idea, illustrate it and turn it into a simple visual story that can help clarify the core concept as well as its use case.
