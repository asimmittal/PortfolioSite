Multitouch++

Improving multitouch interactions on Android


Skills

  • Software Development
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Android SDK

Problem

In the earlier versions of Android (v2.2), touch events didn't bind accurately to the interactors being rendered on the screen. While the device could detect multiple fingers on the screen, the framework didn't allow developers to accurately bind a touch point to a specific interactor on the display.

Solution



Over the course of a month, I built a sample application that could render multiple interactors on the screen, and accurately resolve which finger was touching which view. This framework allowed interactors to be manipulated independently. I also proposed use cases for interactions that involved more than two fingers.
