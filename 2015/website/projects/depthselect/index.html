DepthSelect

Using pressure to resolve pick ambiguity


Skills

  • Software Development
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Interaction design

Goal

This was a concept created for the UIST Innovation contest (2012). We were given a pressure sensitive trackpad and asked to come up with an interesting use case for it.

Solution



Designers often spend a lot of time working with layers when creating digital artifacts and a lot of that time is spent navigating between layers. Our idea was to leverage change in pressure on the track pad to allow designers to peek through layers that occlude one another and finally reduce the time spent in trying to access a particular layer.
