Coolstone

Turn your phone into a multi-touch trackpad


Skills

  • Software Development
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Interaction design
  • Android SDK

Problem

We spend a large part of our time working on computers everyday. But today's interfaces, don't allow us to realize our full productivity. Do we really need to remember awkard keyboard shortcuts to do everyday tasks? They also lack contextual awareness and knowledge of presence. Why does my computer not know that I'm not at my terminal anymore? This project is an attempt to address some of these issues.

Solution



Modern smartphones are equipped with multi-touch displays and accelerometers. Multi touch interaction offers many possibilities to improve everyday tasks such as navigation, organization and examination of content. Other sensors in the phone can help establish some sense of context. In this prototype, I've used multi-touch gestures to simplify navigation between desktops, allow for content magnification and used the accelerometer data on the phone to facilitate logging out.
