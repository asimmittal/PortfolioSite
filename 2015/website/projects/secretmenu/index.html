The Big Idea

Emirates was looking for ideas to help engage its customers in interesting ways. Most food search apps (e.g. yelp, Eat24 etc.) are restaurant centric. Secret Menu is "food centric" and allows you to see what other travelers in the area have eaten during their travels to the region you're currently in.

Moreover, much of Emirates' clientele hails from Asia and the middle East, regions where people have stronger dietary preferences and restrictions. Secret Menu not only allows you to find local food in the region, but also indicates whether a dish was vegetarian, kosher or halal and how much it costs. This idea took a winning spot at the Emirates Travel Hackathon held in Mountain View (2013).

Features

Over the course of 24 hours, I designed the app flow, the UI and built an interactive prototype of the app on the Windows Phone platform. Here were some of the key features of the protoype:

  • Taking a picture of your food, tagging it with meta data
  • Discovering food nearby
  • Liking others' food selection
  • Making reservations at Emirates' partner restaurants
  • Sharing your secret menu with others
